Chef Luca Romano

Born on the Amalfi Coast, Chef Luca brings the soul of Italian coastal cooking to Amara. With training in Michelin-starred kitchens across Italy and France, his philosophy is simple: let the ingredients speak. Every dish celebrates the bounty of the sea, the richness of the land, and the warmth of Italian hospitality.

At Amara, we invite you to experience a culinary journey where tradition meets innovation — from handmade pasta to wood-fired seafood, all served in an atmosphere of understated luxury.

— Luca Romano, Chef & Patron
